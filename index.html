About EduTech Academy

EduTech Academy was founded by Anum Daniel Laryea, a visionary educator dedicated to modernizing learning. With a passion for combining traditional school values and cutting-edge technology, Anum has created a platform where students, teachers, and lifelong learners can thrive together.

Under Anum Daniel Laryea’s leadership, EduTech Academy integrates classroom education, online courses, and insightful blogs, making learning accessible, interactive, and future-ready.

From primary school classes to advanced courses, Anum ensures that every learner has the tools, guidance, and inspiration to succeed in a fast-evolving world.
